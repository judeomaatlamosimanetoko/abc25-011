Our Sneaker Story

More than just shoes — we're building a community of sneaker lovers across Botswana.

The Jom's Journey

Jom's Sneakers was born in 2020 from a simple belief: everyone deserves to step into premium quality footwear without breaking the bank. What started as a small passion project in Gaborone has grown into one of Botswana's most trusted sneaker destinations.

Our founder, Jom, realized that sneaker lovers in Botswana struggled to find authentic, trendy footwear at fair prices. Long shipping times, questionable authenticity, and limited options were the norm. So we changed the game.

Today, we partner directly with authorized distributors to bring you 100% genuine sneakers from Nike, Adidas, Puma, Jordan, New Balance, and more. Every pair is hand-inspected before it reaches your doorstep.

Our Mission

To democratize access to authentic premium sneakers across Botswana by offering competitive prices, exceptional customer service, and a seamless shopping experience.

Our Vision

To become the most loved sneaker retailer in Southern Africa — where style, authenticity, and community come together.

Our Promise

100% authentic products, transparent pricing, fast delivery across Botswana, and a hassle-free return policy. Your trust is our greatest asset.
