Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?
Within Gaborone: 1-2 business days. Other regions in Botswana: 3-5 business days. You'll receive tracking info via SMS/email.
Can I return or exchange sneakers?
Yes! Unworn, original condition sneakers can be returned within 14 days. Free exchanges for size issues. Contact support for a return label.
Are the sneakers 100% authentic?
Absolutely. We source directly from official brand distributors. Every pair is verified before shipping.
